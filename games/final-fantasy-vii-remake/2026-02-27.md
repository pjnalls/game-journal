### Literary Analysis

From the very beginning of the game, we're shown a bird flying from a dried-up, desert-like land surrounding Midgar, which is the city of this game, into which the bird flies.

This is symbolic of just how much devastation the overuse of mako energy, the life force of the Planet, can cause in this world.

**Life**... It's the central theme of Final Fantasy VII (FF7) in the game. 
We see particularly how not only protecting human lives, but all life on the Planet matters so much to certain key characters and even the creators of the FF7 series themselves.

Naturally, there would be conflict between those who want to exploit and harm this **Life** and those who want to protect and care for it.

So, here we are in the first chapter... an ecoterrorist group working with an ex-mercenary to save the Planet by questionable means.
